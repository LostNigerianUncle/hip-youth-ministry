Setting Goals for the Future: A Hip Youth Ministry Vlog

In this Hip Youth Ministry Vlog, we discuss the importance of setting goals for the future and what Jesus would say about our ambition. Everyone has to have a plan of what they want to do in the future, therefore it is important that we must come together and talk about our ambitions to understand how we can achieve them.

Hip Youth Ministry acknowledges the traditional custodians of the Ngunnawal country in which this website is based. We pay our respects to the elders past, present, and future. We remember that this land is, was, and always will be traditional First Nation's land.
